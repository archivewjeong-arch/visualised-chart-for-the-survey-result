About Tagged

eSafety Commissioner, 2025

Tagged is a 19-minute film about cyberbullying escalating to police involvement, accompanied by structured student worksheets. Students work through worksheets that require them to identify cyberbullying behaviour, analyse bystander roles, evaluate characters' choices, and develop personal strategies for reporting and responding to online abuse.

Academic Justification

Jones and Mitchell (2016)

Online respect and civic engagement are negatively associated with harassment and positively associated with helpful bystander behaviour — both developed through Tagged's worksheet sequence.

Falloon (2020) — TDC Framework

Personal-ethical digital competence requires explicit progressive scaffolding. Tagged provides this by moving students from identification (Worksheet 1) to consequence analysis (Worksheets 3 and 4), to evaluative reporting (Worksheet 5), to personal action planning (Worksheet 6).

Puentedura (2013) — SAMR
Modification

Cyberbullying education is redesigned through the combined activities, enabling students to analyse real-time character decisions at specific timestamps while completing structured civic reflection activities.
